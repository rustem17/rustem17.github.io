Hello! I'm Rustem, and this is my personal page.

My research focuses on AI alignment and scalable oversight. I'm currently a Research Scholar at MATS in London, working with Prof. Shi Feng on the realism of model organisms of misalignment and the science of generalization. Another focus is applying invariance to alignment problems. I'm also interested in developing counterpart domain randomization as a method for AI control. Previously, I was a Research Engineer at Palisade Research, where I evaluated LLM agents in offensive security settings and studied how they misbehave. I'm also interested in curriculum learning and evolutionary algorithms.

Outside research, I care a lot about music. One day I'd love to build an AI — or simply a tool — that helps compose or discover great new music, with the richness of the Romantic and Baroque traditions.
